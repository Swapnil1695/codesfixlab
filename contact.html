Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will I get a response?

We aim to respond to all emails within 24-48 hours. For instant coding help, use our AI Code Helper which is available 24/7.

Can you help me debug my code?

Yes! Use our Code Tester to test your code, or ask our AI Code Helper for debugging assistance. For complex issues, email us with your code and error messages.

Do you offer tutoring or mentorship?

While we don't offer one-on-one tutoring, our Mini Projects and Free Source Code are designed to be self-guided learning resources with step-by-step instructions.

Can I contribute to CodeFixLab?

We welcome contributions! If you have code examples, project ideas, or error fixes to share, please email us with your suggestions. We're always looking to expand our resources.
